CATTLE MONITORING APPLICATION


For any progressive as well as progress countries Agriculture play crucial role or we can say that it’s act like backbone of such countries.

• At present agriculture besides farming includes forestry, fruit cultivation, dairy, poultry, Bee keeping etc. today, all accepted as part of modern agriculture.

• Out of this scale of dairy farming has been changed worldwide over recent year with a move toward larger, more intensive, profit driven enterprises, primarily due to market pressure and demanding of milk products.

• The income of the farmer’s mainly depend on the cattle’s health condition as most of them depend on the dairy products for their living.

• Majority of the dairy farm contain large number of cattle’s and there is deformity in health monitoring aspects. Hence it is difficult for the farmers to monitor the health parameters with the standard reference healthy parameters.
So through the cattle monitoring system they can monitor the health of the cattle based on the parameters such as foot step, temperature, blood pressure, heart beat, respiration and body humidity
